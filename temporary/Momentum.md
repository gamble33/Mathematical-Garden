$$p=mv$$
Where:
- $p$ is momentum ($kgms^{-1}$)
- $m$ is [[Mass|mass]] ($kg$)
- $v$ is [[Velocity|velocity]] ($ms^{-1}$)

# Kinetic Energy Relation to Momentum
$$E_k=\frac{p^2}{2m}$$
## Derivation
$$E_k=\frac{1}{2}mv^2$$
$$p=mv\rightarrow v=\frac{p}{m}$$
$$\rightarrow E_k=\frac{1}{2}m\frac{p^2}{m^2}=\frac{p^2m}{2m^2}=\frac{p^2}{2m}$$
