# Boolean Algebra
---
## Basics
Any value or [[Variable|variable]] in Boolean algebra is either $1$ (true) or $0$ (false).
**Example operations**
$$1+1=1$$
$$1+0=1$$
$$0+0=0$$
$$1.0=0$$
## Laws
### Identity
$$1.A=A$$
$$0+A=A$$
### Null
$$0.A=0$$
$$1+A=1$$
### Idempotent
$$A.A=A$$
$$A+A=A$$
### Inverse
$$A.{\overline A}=0$$
$$A+{\overline A}=1$$
### Commutative
$$A.B=B.A$$
$$A+B=B+A$$
### Associative
$$(A.B).C=A.(B.C)$$
$$(A+B)+C=A+(B+C)$$
### Distributive
$$A+B.C=(A+B).(A+C)$$
$$A.(B+C)=A.B+A.C$$
### Absorption
$$A.(A+B)=A$$
$$A+A.B=A$$
### De Morgan's
$${\overline {A.B}} = {\overline A} + {\overline{B}}$$
$$\overline{A+B}=\overline{A}.\overline{B}$$
### Double Complement
$$\overline{\overline{A}}=A$$
